The Story of Sirtaki

The Sirtaki was created by composer Mikis Theodorakis for the 1964 film "Zorba the Greek." This captivating dance combines two traditional Greek dances:

  • Hasapiko - The slow, graceful beginning (0-40 seconds)
  • Hasaposerviko - The fast, energetic finale (80-120 seconds)

What makes Sirtaki unique is its progressive tempo - starting slow at 60 BPM and building to an exhilarating 130 BPM crescendo that captures the spirit of Greek celebration.
