Essential Sailing Tips

Best Sailing Season

May, June, September, and October offer fewer crowds and more manageable winds. July-August brings strong Meltemi winds.

Essential Equipment

Bring snorkeling gear and hiking shoes - many islets offer both marine and land adventures.

Protected Areas

Monitor for restrictions - many beaches and caves are protected habitats or archaeological sites.

Wildlife Watching

Look for dolphins, monk seals, Eleonora's falcons, and rare endemic plants in Natura 2000 areas.
